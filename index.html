Welcome to Civic Duty:Comedic Warfare

CD:CW is a party for 4-10 players where points are awarded based on your votes! The player with the most votes/points in each round is appointed champion. There are many unique games, but remember to get out and vote.

Pick-tionary

Type: Word, Solo

Players come up with a definition of the prompt and vote on their favourite definition.

Catchy-phrase Goes Here

Type: Word, Solo

Players create a catchy phrase related to the prompt. Players vote on their favourite.

Storybored

Type: Word, Solo

Players must quickly write a short story with the prompts and vote on their favourite definition.

Memory Is The Key

Type: Draw, Competitive

One player draws via the prompt and another has the ability to erase for 3 seconds after they have finished. Points are based on how many correct guesses.

Picture Story

Type: Drawing, Solo

Each player draws one sketch that encompasses three prompts. The artist selects the guess that best describes the drawing or is just funny.

Drawversus

Type: Drawing, Head to Head

Groups of two must draw similar prompts. The audience must guess which is which. For each correct guess the artist and guesser receive points.

Draw-op

Type: Drawing, Cooperative

Groups of two must cooperatively complete a drawing. Only one player can draw at one time.

Reverse Pick-tionary

Type: Word, Solo

Players come up with a the word that is described from its definition and vote on their favourite definition.

We Can Finish Each Others...

Type: Word, Co-op

Groups of two must submit a word one letter at a time alternating between players to answer a question.

Children's Story, Horrible Crime, or Dessert?

Type: Guess, Solo

One player draws via the prompt. Other players must guess which category it fits under.
